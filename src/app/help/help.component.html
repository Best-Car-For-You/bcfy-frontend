Help Page

Welcome to our help page! Here's a step-by-step guide on how to find your perfect car:

  1. Start by selecting the make and model of the car you are interested in.
  2. Next, set your budget range by specifying the minimum and maximum price you are willing to pay.
  3. Click on the "Search" button to find cars that match your criteria.
  4. Once the search results are displayed, you can browse through the available cars and their price ranges.
  5. If you find a car that you are interested in, click on it to view more details.
  6. Feel free to use the filtering options to narrow down your search results based on additional criteria such as mileage, year, or location.
  7. If you have any further questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.

We hope this guide helps you in finding your perfect car. Happy car hunting!
